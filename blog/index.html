Pieniny

Cousine:
Pieniny offers delicious combinations of potatoes, grains, mushrooms, and especially sheep cheese. It's worth visiting shepherds' hut for regional delicacies, especially Oscypki. Local specialties include ga≈Çuszki (dumplings) with bryndza and bacon, cold soup from Sromowce and Czorsztyn goulash.
Bikes:
It's worth renting a bike and taking a special trip along the "Droga Pieninska", which runs alongside the Dunajec River to the 14th-century Red Monastery on the Slovak side. The round trip from Szczawnica takes about 2.5 hours.
